# KON-NECT — Discover Real Connections, Right Where You Are

**KON-NECT** is a real-time social discovery platform that helps you find and connect with people near you who share your interests. Open the map, see who's around, send a request, and start a real conversation — no algorithms, no endless feeds.

---

## What KON-NECT Does

Most social apps show you people from anywhere in the world. KON-NECT flips that — it starts with the people near you right now. If you share interests with someone within 20km, you'll see them on the map. From there, you can connect, navigate to them, and chat in real time.

---

## Features

### 🗺️ Live Social Map
The heart of KON-NECT is an interactive map that shows real people near you as colored pins. Your location updates live as you move. Pins are color-coded:
- **Green** — people with shared interests nearby
- **Red / Purple** — your friends (changes based on your theme)
- **Gray** — others in the area

Click any pin to see their name, bio, shared interests, and connection options. Right-click anywhere on the map to drop a pin for navigation.

### 🔍 Smart Discovery
KON-NECT automatically finds people within 20km who share at least one of your interests and ranks them by how many interests you have in common. You can also switch to **Global View** to explore users worldwide sorted by match level.

### 🧠 Interest Matching & Scoring
Every user profile has a list of interests — hobbies, passions, activities. The app compares your interests against everyone near you and assigns a **match score**. Higher scores mean more things in common. Shared interests are highlighted with a ★ star on their profile.

### 🏷️ Custom Interests
You're not limited to a preset list. You can add your own custom interest tags beyond the 12 standard categories. Custom interests are automatically capitalized and deduplicated. If a tag already exists across the platform, the existing version is used to keep things consistent.

### 🤝 Friend Requests & Connections
- Send a friend request to anyone you find on the map or in the Connect tab
- If both users send requests to each other, they are automatically connected
- You can **cancel** a sent request at any time
- You can **accept** or **decline** incoming requests
- Once connected, you can **unfriend** at any time from their profile or the map

### 💬 Persistent Encrypted Chat
Chat is available exclusively with friends (mutual connections). All messages are:
- **Encrypted** before being saved — your conversations are always private
- **Stored** so you never lose your chat history when you close the app
- **Read receipts** — you can see when your friend has read your message (similar to blue ticks)
- Live unread counts are shown on your friends list

### 🚗 Turn-by-Turn Navigation
Click **Directions** on any user's profile or dropped pin to get a driving route. The navigation line dynamically trims behind you as you move, so you always see the remaining path. The app also alerts you if the route stretches beyond road coverage (e.g. across a sea).

### 📍 Dropped Pins & Place Search
- Type any place in the search bar to navigate to it instantly, and the app automatically drops a named pin there
- Right-click anywhere on the map to drop a custom navigation pin with the address auto-filled from the location

### 📊 Dashboard Stats
Your home screen shows live stats about your area:
- How many of your friends are active near you
- How many of your distinct interests are shared by nearby users
- **Trending Near You** — the top 5 most popular interests within 20km, updated live

### 🛡️ Safety & Privacy
- Your **exact location is never exposed** — only a general area is shown to non-friends
- **Online status** is visible only to mutual friends
- You can **block** any user, which also removes them from your map, friend list, and all future discovery

### 🌗 Dark & Light Mode
Full dark mode and light mode support with automatic theme detection and smooth transitions. The map and all UI elements adapt to your chosen theme.

### 💡 Easter Egg
Type `SHOW-CLUSTER-CENTERS` into the search bar to reveal an interest-based clustering overlay on the map — showing you visual hotspots of people grouped by their primary interest.

## How to Get Started

1. **Sign up** with your email or continue with Google
2. **Set your interests** — pick from the standard list or add your own custom tags
3. **Open the map** — you'll see people near you with shared interests immediately
4. **Send a friend request** from the map or the Connect tab
5. **Start chatting** once they accept

---

## Privacy First

KON-NECT was built with privacy as a core principle — not an afterthought. Your location is only used to surface nearby people; it is never sold or shared. Chat is encrypted end-to-end. Blocking is instant and absolute.
